Methodology

This simulation employs the **FP-Growth algorithm** to mine frequent itemsets from user purchase data. Unlike simpler methods, FP-Growth avoids costly candidate generation, offering a highly efficient way to discover product associations. Rules are generated from these itemsets and evaluated using Support, Confidence, and Lift metrics to ensure relevance and strength.
